About Us

01

Why Choose us

Edumark School realized the difficult circumstances facing our students during their university studies and after they graduated from the university, and realized that the graduate It must be special to be able to serve himself, his family, his community and his homeland, as I realized the size of the financial difficulties that parents face in providing material requirements for their children during their studies, so the university set its sights as a primary goal that does not exceed any other goal, namely: obtaining a university graduate He is distinguished by knowledge, knowledge and application, possessing culture and communication skills, which makes him an active individual able to interact positively with his colleagues and society.

02

Our Mission

The Edumark School has placed its highest priority on ensuring its students in both scientific and applied terms, creating all the scientific requirements for them (distinguished professors, effective academic guidance, advanced laboratories, ...), and they have been given the opportunity to practice decent and beneficial student activities, in addition to providing a package of services Class that will enable its children to study and achieve in a fertile environment suitable for academic excellence. At the same time, the university has set its sights on the issue of qualitative upgrading of the level of education, by strict adherence to the reliability controls established by the Ministry of Higher Education, and to benefit from the expertise of prestigious foreign, regional and local universities

03

Our vission

To be a leading institution in higher education and scientific research with a prominent reputation at the local, regional and international levels, and to achieve all local and international accreditation conditions, and to be one of the main pillars for achieving the national goals of higher education.
